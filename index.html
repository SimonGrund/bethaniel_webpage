Why Betty

A private copy editor on your machine

Everything a great copy editor should be. Now free, private, and right there on your own computer.

Completely private

Your words never leave your computer. No cloud, no servers, no subscription. Betty runs entirely on your machine — what you write is yours, full stop.

Built for writers

Betty understands prose. She catches the usual grammar gremlins, yes — but she also notices when a sentence is doing too much work, or not enough.

One-click setup

Download, open, write. No configuration files, no command line, no API keys. If you can open a document, you can use Betty.

No data centres

Betty runs entirely on your machine. No cloud server humming away somewhere on your behalf, no distant data centre, no someone else’s electricity. What runs, runs on yours.

Free. Actually free.

No trial period. No freemium tier. No credit card required. Betty is free because good writing tools should be available to everyone who writes.

Your voice, kept

Betty suggests — she doesn't overwrite. Every edit is yours to accept, reject, or argue about. The words stay yours from first draft to final full stop.

Getting started

Four steps. Then just write.

1

Download

Grab the installer for your system above. It's one file — no package manager, no dependencies to chase down.

2

Install

Open the installer and follow along. First thing you have to do, once Betty is installed, is select the size of Betty you think your machine can handle. Don't worry, you can always change your mind later.

3

Open your writing

Add your manuscript and a description of the style you're aiming for. Betty works with plain text, Markdown, and most common document formats. With the style document, you can give custom instructions such as "Don't correct the pirates. They are supposed to talk like that."

4

Let her read

Hit edit and Betty will go through your work. Suggestions come up for you to evaluate — accept them, ignore them, your choice.

A few notes

What Betty is — and isn't.

She's good. But she's not magic. Here's what you should know before you start.

Read the full disclosure

Betty is a tool, not a professional. She is a writing aid — not a qualified copy editor, proofreader, or legal reviewer. Her suggestions are starting points, not verdicts. The final call on every sentence is yours.

She will sometimes be wrong. Language is context-dependent in ways that are genuinely hard. Betty will occasionally flag something that was fine, miss something that wasn’t, or suggest a change that makes things worse. Treat her output as a second opinion, not a final edit.

Nothing leaves your machine. Betty processes text entirely on your own hardware. No part of what you write is sent to any server, stored in any cloud, shared with any third party, or used to train anything. The text you put in stays in. Period.

Betty requires capable hardware. She needs at least 8 GB of RAM and approximately 3 GB of free disk space. On older or lower-spec machines she may run slowly or not at all. She does real computational work — there is no lightweight version.

Supported platforms. Betty is currently available for macOS 12 or later, Windows 10 (64-bit) or later, and Linux systems running glibc 2.31 or newer. Other platforms are not supported at this time.

Betty is free, and intends to stay that way. There is no paid tier, no usage limit, no subscription, and no trial period. If that ever changes, it will be announced here before it takes effect — with enough notice to make a considered decision.

No warranties. Betty is provided as-is. The developers make no guarantees about accuracy, fitness for any particular purpose, or uninterrupted availability. Use at your own discretion.
