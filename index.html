Why Betty

Everything a great copy editor should be. None of what you'd rather skip.

Completely private

Your words never leave your computer. No cloud, no servers, no subscription. Betty runs entirely on your machine — what you write is yours, full stop.

Built for writers

Betty understands prose. She catches the usual grammar gremlins, yes — but she also notices when a sentence is doing too much work, or not enough.

One-click setup

Download, open, write. No configuration files, no command line, no API keys. If you can open a document, you can use Betty.

No data centres

Betty runs entirely on your machine. No cloud server humming away somewhere on your behalf, no distant data centre, no someone else’s electricity. What runs, runs on yours.

Free. Actually free.

No trial period. No freemium tier. No credit card required. Betty is free because good writing tools should be available to everyone who writes.

Your voice, kept

Betty suggests — she doesn't overwrite. Every edit is yours to accept, reject, or argue about. The words stay yours from first draft to final full stop.

Getting started

Four steps. Then just write.

1

Download

Grab the installer for your system above. It's one file — no package manager, no dependencies to chase down.

2

Install

Open the installer and follow along. The whole thing takes about as long as making a cup of tea. Maybe less.

3

Open your writing

Paste in your text, or open a file directly. Betty works with plain text, Markdown, and most common document formats.

4

Let her read

Hit edit and Betty will go through your work. Suggestions appear inline — accept them, ignore them, your choice. She can take it.

A few notes

What Betty is — and isn't.

She's good. But she's not magic. Here's what you should know before you start.

Read the full disclosure

Betty is a writing aid, not a ghostwriter. She suggests edits — you decide what to keep. Your voice, your call, always.

She can be wrong. Language is strange and context is everything. Betty will occasionally suggest something that isn't better than what you had. Read her suggestions; don't just accept them in a batch.

No data is sent anywhere. Betty runs a language model entirely on your device. Nothing you type is transmitted to any server, logged, or stored outside your own machine.

System requirements apply. Betty needs at least 8 GB of RAM and around 3 GB of free disk space. On older or lower-spec machines she may run slowly — she's doing real computational work under the hood.

Betty is free. There is no paid tier, no upsell, no trial period ending. If that ever changes, you'll hear about it here first — in Betty's own words.
